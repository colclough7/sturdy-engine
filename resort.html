Taniti Beach Resort

★★★★★
PoolSpaRestaurantBeach AccessGym

The Taniti Beach Resort is the island's premier accommodation, sitting right on the edge of Taniti's most pristine bay. Every room faces the ocean, and the resort's private beach gives guests direct access to some of the best snorkelling waters in the Pacific.

Whether you are looking to unwind at the spa, dine on fresh local seafood at Coral Table restaurant, or simply watch the sunset from your balcony, the resort makes it effortless to do absolutely nothing — or absolutely everything.

Resort Amenities

☂

Pool & Spa

Lounge by the infinity pool overlooking the bay, or book a treatment at our full-service spa offering traditional Polynesian massages and body wraps.

⛤

Coral Table Restaurant

Our signature restaurant serves fresh Pacific seafood and locally grown produce daily. Breakfast is included with every stay, and dinner reservations are strongly recommended.

◱

Private Beach Access

Step directly from the resort onto Taniti's most sheltered beach. Snorkelling gear, kayaks, and paddleboards are available to hire at the beach hut throughout the day.
