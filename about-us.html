Our Story

FakeRoads Tours was founded in 2010 by a group of Taniti locals who wanted to share their island with the world on their own terms. What started as a small snorkelling and hiking operation has grown into Taniti's most trusted full-service tour company, welcoming thousands of visitors every year.

We believe travel should be personal. Every tour we run, every room we recommend, and every restaurant we send you to has been personally experienced by a member of our team. We do not list anything we would not enjoy ourselves.

Our Values

♥

Local First

Every guide, driver, cook, and host we work with is a Taniti local. We are committed to keeping tourism revenue within the island community and supporting the people who call this place home.

★

Honest Experiences

We do not oversell. We tell you exactly what to expect from each tour, accommodation, and restaurant so you can make decisions that are right for you — not just for our commission.

●

Protecting Taniti

We operate with the island's future in mind. Our tours follow sustainable trail guidelines, we partner with eco-certified accommodation, and we actively support local marine conservation efforts.
