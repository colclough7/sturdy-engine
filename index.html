About Us

Explore The Difference

Taniti is a small but stunning tropical island in the Pacific, home to white sand beaches, a volcanic interior, and warm, welcoming locals. Whether you are looking for adventure or relaxation, this hidden gem has something for every kind of traveller.

FakeRoads Tours has been connecting visitors with the best of Taniti since 2010. We handle everything accommodation, activities, dining, and transport so you can focus on making memories.
